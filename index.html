Restore Physical Therapy focuses on the use of the gentle positional fascia release technique of (Jones Strain/Fascial) Counterstrain to identify and treat the simple to the complex of conditions working to restore the homeostatic state of the entire body as it relates to disrupted daily function. Untreated dysfunctions of systems within the body can lead to layers of chronic pain presentations that can be hard to break the cycle and unpeel the layers of guarding/spasm/inflammation. This often leave a person in a helpless, frustrated, depressive state with no answers or relief despite seeing many different physicians and clinicians in many different fields. We feel fortunate to have over 30+ years of hands on skills and experience that have been able to find relief and restoration of quality of life when many times hope had been lost.
